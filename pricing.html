Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is this just a chatbot?

No, this is a complete AI employee system. Unlike chatbots that give rule-based responses, our AI makes intelligent decisions like a real employee. It learns from interactions and improves over time.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are fully operational within 3-7 days. Complex integrations may take a bit longer, but we handle everything for you.

What languages does the AI support?

Hindi, English, and Gujarati - all included. The AI naturally switches between languages based on customer preference.

Is my data secure?

We use bank-grade encryption and are GDPR compliant. Your business data is completely secure and never shared with third parties.

Can I customize the AI for my business?

Yes, fully customizable. We configure the AI based on your specific business processes, terminology, and workflows.

Why is there a setup fee?

The setup fee covers AI training (teaching the system about your business), customization (building workflows), and integration (connecting with your tools). This is a one-time investment that pays for itself within 2-3 months through savings and increased revenue.
