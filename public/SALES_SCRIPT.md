## 📞 CALL SCRIPT FOR CLOSING ₹1L+ DEALS

### PHASE 1: OPENING (30 seconds)

**Script:**
> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from ALGO IQ. How are you doing today?"
> 
> "I help businesses automate their sales, support, and operations using AI employees. Have you explored any AI solutions for your business yet?"

**Goal:** Break ice, gauge awareness

---

### PHASE 2: DISCOVERY (2 minutes)

**Questions to Ask:**
1. "Tell me about your business - what industry are you in?"
2. "How big is your team currently?"
3. "What's your biggest challenge right now - is it generating leads, managing customers, or something else?"
4. "How much are you currently spending on staff for [sales/support/operations]?"
5. "If I could show you a way to get 3x results at the same cost, would you be interested?"

**Listen for:** Pain points, budget indicators, decision timeline

---

### PHASE 3: PRESENT SOLUTION (3 minutes)

**Script:**
> "Based on what you've shared, here's what I'd recommend..."
> 
> "We have 13 specialized AI employees. For your situation, I'd suggest starting with [recommended AI agents]."
> 
> "Let me explain what each one does:
> - **[ARJUN/Sales AI]:** Handles lead capture, auto follow-ups, WhatsApp integration - converts 2-5x more leads
> - **[VIDUR/Support AI]:** 24/7 customer support on WhatsApp and website - handles 80% of queries automatically
> - **[KRISHNA/Intelligence AI]:** Handles complex queries that Vidur can't - acts as L2 support"
> 
> "The investment is ₹[X] per month plus a one-time setup fee of ₹[Y]. The setup fee covers training the AI on your business, building workflows, and integration."

---

### PHASE 4: ROI JUSTIFICATION (2 minutes)

**Script:**
> "Let me show you the math..."
> 
> | Factor | Traditional Staff | AI System |
> |--------|-------------------|-----------|
> | Monthly Cost | ₹30,000+ | ₹25,000-50,000 |
> | Work Hours | 8 hrs/day | 24x7 |
> | Leave Days | 52+/year | Zero |
> | Productivity | 1x | 3x |
> 
> "So you're actually saving money while getting 3x the output."
> 
> "Most of our clients recover their setup fee within 2 months through savings and increased revenue."

---

### PHASE 5: HANDLE OBJECTIONS

**Objection 1: "Too expensive"**
> Response: "I understand. Let me ask - what's your monthly budget for [sales staff/customer support]? The reason I ask is that our AI actually costs less than hiring one employee, but works 3x harder. Can I show you a breakdown?"
> 
> Alternative: "Let's look at it differently - if this system could generate just 5 more leads per month worth ₹10,000 each, that's ₹50,000 in new revenue. The system costs ₹30,000. Is that ROI worth exploring?"

**Objection 2: "Need to think about it"**
> Response: "Absolutely, this is an important decision. What specifically would you like to think about? Is it the investment, the technology, or whether it's right for your business?"
> 
> "Can we schedule 15 minutes tomorrow to discuss once you've reviewed? I'll send you a quick WhatsApp summary now."

**Objection 3: "We already have a team"**
> Response: "That's great - having a team shows you value personal touch. Here's what I'd suggest: use AI as augmentation, not replacement. Your team handles complex cases, AI handles the volume. This reduces their workload by 80% and lets them focus on high-value tasks."
> 
> "Would your team be happier handling fewer repetitive queries and more meaningful conversations?"

**Objection 4: "How long to set up?"**
> Response: "Most businesses are fully operational within 3-7 days. We handle everything - training, integration, workflow setup. You just approve."
> 
> "Want me to walk you through the process?"

**Objection 5: "Is it secure?"**
> Response: "Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and are GDPR compliant. Your business data never leaves secure servers. We're trusted by 500+ Indian businesses including [relevant examples]."

---

### PHASE 6: CLOSE (1 minute)

**For ₹50K-1L deals:**
> "Great! Let's get you started. I'll send over a quick proposal on WhatsApp now. Which email should I send the formal agreement to?"
> 
> "To lock in today's pricing, we just need a 50% advance. The rest can be paid after successful demo."

**For larger deals:**
> "This sounds like a great fit. Given the scope, I'd recommend a quick 30-minute demonstration showing exactly how it'd work for [their business]. Can we schedule that for tomorrow?"
> 
> "Perfect. I'll send a calendar link over WhatsApp right now."
